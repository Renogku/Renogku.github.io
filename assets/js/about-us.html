About Us

Attend to your own inner health and happiness, Happiness radiates like a fragrance from a flower and draws all good things towards you.

–His Holiness Maharishi Mahesh Yogi

Inspired by the philosophy of H.H. Maharishi Mahesh Yogi to live in harmony with the nature and to provide natural products in their purest, highest quality forms, Narayani Naturals strive to bring to you the best quality products and ingredients.

Narayani Naturals is a family business found in 2013 by Dr. Richa Shrivastava. An expert in medicinal plant sciences and doctorate in standardization of natural products from IIT Delhi. She started with a novel intention of providing raw materials to domestic industry but also began with exports. Our products are powders and mixes of various herbs, spices, cold pressed, blended and special herbal oils. Now we are expanding with addition of plant proteins, cereals, pulses and other food products. India is a treasure trove of natural resources like Medicinal plants, spices and indigenous foods. We are passionate about supporting agro-based industry and understand that we cultivate, standardize and preserve their natural ecosystems.

Mission and Values

We feel that agriculture and industrialization both are important for India’s economic growth and self-sustenance. As a processor of natural products we are responsible for doing this in the best way, by selecting best sustainable sources and by using the best suitable technology to produce an optimum quality product.

All our products are ecologically sourced from farms that are free from heavy metals and we follow and define our own standards and are not bound by any certification system. While dealing in certified organic products we also realize that so many wonderful products come from authentic cultivation where only natural methods are used, due to remote location or lack of technology they are not certified therefore we feel that we must bridge this gap and also process naturally organic products. Therefore our products are ecological quality and free from chemicals and pesticides. We promise great products with love honesty and great quality economical prices. The products undergo stringent quality control during processing at third party labs, thus providing all the relevant documents and analytical reports for guaranted customer satisfaction.

Strategy is to research and delve deep into what makes our customers happy with our products and to keep up with the best technological processes to provide best sustainable and cost effective products. Focus is to provide solutions to various natural product industries dealing in food supplements, herbal cosmetics, teas and oils, looking for natural products that have required attributes and are also free from contamination such as pesticides, trace metals, ETO etc.

As bulk suppliers we always involve in cultivating, processing and exporting products such as herbs, spices, protein powders and oils. Process and pack (ecologically) to preserve their natural attributes such as nutrients, colour, flavour and aroma. We only use food grade packaging to ensure freshness, prevent any contamination and preserve their shelf life. To give uniqueness to every brand, we offer customised processing of desired mesh size & packaging to our partners.
